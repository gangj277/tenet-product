# Product Profile: Lumen

## One-Liner

Lumen is an AI-native research workspace that transforms scattered papers into structured, evidence-grounded synthesis — with tracked claims, surfaced contradictions, and identified gaps.

## The Before/After Transformation

### Before Lumen
- Researcher spends a weekend reading 30+ papers
- Notes scattered across Notion, Google Docs, Zotero, and sticky notes
- Contradictions between papers go unnoticed or are mentally tracked
- Writing a literature review means re-reading everything again
- "What does the evidence actually say?" remains unanswered
- Generic AI summaries hallucinate claims and lack provenance

### After Lumen
- Upload papers or describe a research question → Lumen discovers, ingests, and analyzes sources automatically
- Every claim is traced to a specific source, page, and quote
- Contradictions are explicitly surfaced — not averaged away
- Evidence is organized by confidence level (high/medium/low) and type (supporting/contradictory/methodological)
- An interactive workspace agent helps refine synthesis, challenge assumptions, and identify gaps
- Structured outputs: overview, synthesis, claims map, gaps analysis, next steps

## Target Users (Ranked by Pain Intensity)

### Tier 1: Highest Pain (Primary CBT targets)
1. **Systematic review authors** — Literally their job to synthesize evidence. Currently takes months of manual work.
2. **PhD students in literature review phase** — Drowning in papers, struggling to articulate "what the field says"
3. **Postdoctoral researchers** — Need to rapidly survey new fields when shifting research focus

### Tier 2: High Pain
4. **Research consultants / think tank analysts** — Paid to deliver evidence-based recommendations under deadline
5. **Medical/clinical researchers** — Lives depend on getting the evidence synthesis right
6. **Policy researchers** — Need to ground policy recommendations in solid evidence

### Tier 3: Moderate Pain (Future expansion)
7. **R&D teams at companies** — Technology landscape analysis, competitive intelligence
8. **Graduate students (coursework)** — Term papers, thesis proposals
9. **Science journalists** — Need to quickly understand "what the research says"

## Product Stage

**Pre-launch / Private Beta** — Working product, no public users yet. Full pipeline operational (discovery → synthesis → workspace agent).

## Key Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Multi-source discovery | Searches OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, and arXiv simultaneously with deduplication |
| PDF ingestion + parsing | Upload your own PDFs or let Lumen discover papers. Gemini-powered text extraction |
| Perspective inference | Before searching, Lumen understands your research frame, intent, and evidence criteria |
| Evidence mapping | Every piece of evidence tagged by type, confidence, source, and location |
| Contradiction surfacing | Actively identifies disagreements between sources instead of averaging them |
| Multi-persona analysis | Analytical, skeptical, methodology-focused perspectives during consolidation |
| Structured artifacts | Overview, synthesis, claims, gaps, next-steps — not a blob of text |
| Workspace agent | Interactive AI assistant with tools to read, search, write, and refine workspace files |
| Slash-command skills | `/devils-advocate`, `/methodology-critic`, `/paper-explainer`, `/synthesis-updater` |
| Inline diff proposals | Agent suggests edits via visible diffs; researcher accepts or rejects each change |
| Desktop + Web | Electron app for offline work, Next.js web app for cloud access |

## What Makes Lumen Different

See [Positioning & Messaging](../03-positioning/positioning-and-messaging.md) for the full framework. Key differentiators in brief:

1. **Contradiction surfacing** — No other tool actively finds and highlights disagreements between papers
2. **Evidence provenance** — Every claim traced to source + page + quote + confidence level
3. **Structured judgment, not summaries** — Claims, gaps, next-steps as discrete artifacts, not prose
4. **Perspective-aware** — Understands your research frame before searching
5. **Workspace, not chatbot** — Persistent files you can edit, not a conversation that disappears
