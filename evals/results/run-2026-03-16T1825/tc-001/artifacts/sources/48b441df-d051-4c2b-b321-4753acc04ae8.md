# AI Model Collapse: The Synthetic Data Risk GCs Cannot Ignore

**Publication:** Lexology

**Summary:**
This article highlights the emerging risk of "AI model collapse" for organizations increasingly relying on synthetic data for training AI models. While synthetic data offers cost-effectiveness, its overuse can lead to a significant erosion of model accuracy and an amplification of errors. This degradation poses substantial compliance and reputational risks for businesses, necessitating careful management of data sources in AI development.

**Key Claims:**

*   AI model collapse is a growing business risk.
*   Overreliance on synthetic data for training AI models can degrade their accuracy.
*   Synthetic data can amplify existing errors within AI models.
*   Organizations face compliance and reputational harm due to model collapse.

**Relevant Evidence/Quotes:**

*   "While cost-effective, overreliance can erode accuracy, amplify errors, exposing organisations to compliance and reputational harm."
