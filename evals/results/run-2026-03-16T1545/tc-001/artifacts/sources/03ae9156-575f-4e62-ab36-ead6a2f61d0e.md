# Per-Source Summary: The Curse of Recursion

**Title:** THE CURSE OF RECURSION: Training on Generated Data Makes Models Forget  
**Authors:** Ilia Shumailov, Zakhar Shumaylov, Yiren Zhao, Yarin Gal, Nicolas Papernot, Ross Anderson  
**Publication Info:** arXiv:2305.17493v3 [cs.LG], April 2024 (Original version May 2023)

---

### 1. Concise Summary
This seminal paper identifies and defines "Model Collapse," a degenerative process where generative models trained on data produced by previous models lose the ability to represent the true underlying data distribution. The authors demonstrate that this collapse is nearly inevitable in recursive training loops, characterized first by the disappearance of "tails" (rare events/outcomes) and eventually by the convergence to a low-variance point estimate that bears little resemblance to reality. Through mathematical proofs and experiments with GMMs, VAEs, and LLMs, the study argues that without a continuous influx of original human-generated data, the utility of large-scale web-scraped datasets will rapidly diminish as they become "polluted" by synthetic content.

### 2. Key Claims
*   **Definition of Model Collapse:** A process where model-generated data pollutes the training set of subsequent generations, leading to a misperception of reality.
*   **Two Stages of Decay:** 
    *   *Early Model Collapse:* The model loses information about the tails (low-probability events) of the distribution.
    *   *Late Model Collapse:* The model entangles different modes of the original distribution and converges to a distribution with very small variance (often a delta function).
*   **Primary Cause (Statistical Approximation Error):** Arises because the number of samples is finite; at each generation, there is a non-zero probability of losing information about rare states during re-sampling.
*   **Secondary Cause (Functional Approximation Error):** Arises from the limitations of the model architecture (e.g., a neural network's inability to perfectly map a complex distribution), which can introduce noise or non-zero likelihood in incorrect regions.
*   **Inevitability:** Even under "ideal" conditions (perfect functional approximation), statistical sampling error alone is sufficient to trigger collapse over time.
*   **Value of Human Data:** As the internet becomes saturated with AI-generated content, genuine human-produced data becomes increasingly valuable for maintaining model performance.

### 3. Relevant Evidence or Quotes
*   **The "Delta Function" Convergence:** "The only absorbing states are those corresponding to delta functions. As a result... we are guaranteed to end up in a constant state, having lost all the information of the original distribution."
*   **Empirical LLM Decay:** In an experiment with OPT-125m, by Generation 9, a prompt about church architecture resulted in a nonsensical repetitive list of "black-tailed jackrabbits, white-tailed jackrabbits..."
*   **Mathematical Risk:** The authors derive that the variance of the estimated distribution diverges linearly over generations ($n \to \infty$), similar to a Gaussian random walk.
*   **Data Mixing:** "To avoid model collapse, access to genuine human-generated content is essential." The paper notes that while accumulating data in a pool (mixing old and new) slows the process, it does not fundamentally eliminate the drift without fresh grounding.

### 4. Contribution to Research Frame
*   **Technical Validity:** This source provides the foundational mathematical proof that model collapse is a fundamental risk of recursive training, supporting the "inevitability" side of the research project's inquiry.
*   **Mechanisms of Shift:** It explicitly distinguishes between *statistical* (sampling) and *functional* (architectural) errors, providing a framework for the researcher to categorize mitigation strategies.
*   **Challenges to Mitigation:** The paper suggests that even "perfect" models (no functional error) will eventually collapse due to the finite nature of sampling, setting a high bar for engineering solutions that claim to "solve" collapse through architecture alone.
*   **Data Hygiene:** It reinforces the hypothesis that "data poisoning" from synthetic sources is a primary driver of degradation, validating the research focus on the ratio of real-to-synthetic data.
